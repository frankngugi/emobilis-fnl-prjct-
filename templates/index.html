About Our Church

Welcome to Redeemed Gospel Church! We are a vibrant and inclusive Christian community in [Nairobi, Kenya] where all are welcome to explore faith, connect with others, and grow in their relationship with God.

come get to know us

  • Our Mission: To build bridges of love, acceptance, and compassion within our community, reflecting the teachings of Jesus Christ.
  • Our Values:We believe in grace, inclusivity, and service. We offer a judgment-free space where everyone can feel welcome, regardless of background or beliefs. We are passionate about serving our community and making a positive difference in the lives of others
  • What We Believe: We are a non-denominational church grounded in the core teachings of Christianity, emphasizing the importance of love, forgiveness, and service.
Our Worship Services: Our Sunday services are a blend of contemporary music, inspiring messages, and opportunities for reflection and connection. We offer childcare and age-appropriate activities for children during the service. Whether you're new to faith or a seasoned believer, you'll find a warm welcome at Redeemed Gospel Church.
Our Ministries: We offer a variety of ministries to connect with others and serve our community, including a young adult group, a community outreach program, and a weekly Bible study. We invite you to join us for a service, explore our website, or reach out to learn more about our church. We look forward to getting to know you!
