About Redeemed Gospel Church Nyahururu

A vibrant, Spirit-filled Christian community in the heart of Nyahururu, Laikipia County — where everyone is welcomed and valued.

  • Our Mission: To build bridges of love, acceptance, and compassion, reflecting the teachings of Jesus Christ.
  • Our Vision: A community where every soul experiences the transforming grace of God.
  • Our Values: Grace, Inclusivity, Service, Faith, and Community.
  • What We Believe: Grounded in the core teachings of Christianity — love, forgiveness, and service.

Sunday Services: 9:00 AM every Sunday. All are welcome — regardless of background or beliefs. We offer Sunday School for children and vibrant worship for the whole family.

Our Ministries: Men's Ministry, Women's Ministry, Youth Fellowship, Sunday School, Choir, and Community Outreach.
